Flagship smartphone with 5.7" display, 13MP dual-camera

5.7" full HD display, slim and light
Full metal body with 2.5D curved glass

Mi 5s Plus comes with a large display that doesn't overwhelm. Beneath the 2.5D curved glass lies a gorgeous 5.7" full HD display.
The back of the phone uses 3D curved metal design, and fits perfect in one palm so you can text, take photos, and browse easily.

13MP dual-camera
Captures the finest of details

The all-new Mi 5s Plus comes with a dual 13MP camera sensors that work together when you take a shot. The standard
RGB sensor captures color details and information, whereas the dedicated monochrome sensor provides a higher contrast,
captures more light,and reduces image noise. So now shots taken with Mi 5s Plus is clearer, better, and more vivid.

Expressing and impressing with finest details

Express and impress with the 13MP+13MP dual camera, which shoots from separate RGB and monochrome
image sensors. Mi 5s Plus stitches both images together in an instant. Photos have higher contrast,
lower noise, and look exceptionally realistic in black and white.

  • Low-light enhancement

Dedicated monochrome sensor
Clearer shots with 2X light sensitivity

The dedicated monochrome sensor doesn't just work like a black and white filter, but adds details and depth to your photographs.
With 2X light sensitivity, the camera sensor captures more light, resulting in brighter and clearer photographs.
