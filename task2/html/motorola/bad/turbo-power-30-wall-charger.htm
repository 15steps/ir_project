    turbopower™ 30 wall charger

    Get a quick burst of power when you need it most

    The 28.5W turbopower 30 wall charger is compatible with USB-C enabled smartphones and tablets, including all moto z phones. It delivers up to 15 hours of power in just 15 minutes of charging.†

    specifications

    Ports

    USB-C plug on attached cable

    Input

    100-240 VAC, 50/60 Hz

    Output

    5V / up to 5.7A under control of Power Delivery compatible device

    Power output

    28.5W

    Power cord length

    4.5 ft (1.3 m)

    Dimensions

    2.0 x 2.2 x 1.3 in (50.5 x 55.2 x 31.8 mm)

    Weight

    4.8 oz (135 g)

    Included in box

    turbopower™ 30 charger
    Warranty card

    Compatibility

    Compatible with USB-C enabled smartphones and tablets, including the moto z family of phones†
