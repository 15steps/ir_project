    it’s time to move on

    It’s time to reinvent your smartphone

    start with a seriously impressive smartphone

    then teach it some new tricks

    moto mods transform your moto z phone into an entirely new device. Think a super-zoom camera, a boombox, a projector and more. Only moto z can do this. Because different is better.

    so how does it work?

    magnets. really strong ones.

    moto mods snap easily onto any moto z phone. They fit perfectly and securely, and you can swap them in a snap.

    snap. tap. done.

    that’s the installation guide

    Snap on a moto mod. Your moto z recognizes it. Tap your phone display to complete the one-time setup. And that’s it. Done. Really.

    future-proof.

    here for the long haul

    Your favorite brands are developing new moto mods right now. And they’ll work with future generation moto z phones too.†
